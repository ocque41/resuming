### Primary Brand Font


- Usage: Headers, marketing materials, brand messaging

### Secondary Brand Font

```css

```

- Usage: Subheaders, accents, pull quotes

### UI Font (Unchanged for Readability)


- Usage: Application interface, form elements, body text

### Font Weights

- Extra Light: 200 (Decorative elements)
- Regular: 400 (Body text)
- Medium: 500 (Subheaders)
- Bold: 700 (Headers)

### Marketing Typography Scale

- Hero Text: 64px/72px (Zen Kaku Gothic New)
- Display: 48px/56px (Zen Old Mincho)
- H1: 40px/48px (Zen Kaku Gothic New)
- H2: 32px/40px (Zen Kaku Gothic New)
- H3: 24px/32px (Zen Kaku Gothic New)
- Body Large: 18px/28px (Zen Old Mincho)
- Body: 16px/24px (Zen Old Mincho)

## 3\. Brand Design Elements

### Iconography

- Style: Brush stroke-inspired line icons
- Weight: 1.5px stroke
- Corners: Slightly rounded to mimic brush endings
- Color: Inherited from brand colors

### Decorative Elements

- Traditional patterns simplified into minimal forms
- Subtle rice paper textures
- Brush stroke accents
- Negative space incorporating Ma (é–“) principle

### Brand Imagery Style

- High contrast photography
- Warm, earthy tones
- Natural textures
- Minimal compositions
- Focus on negative space
- Subtle grain overlay

### Animation Guidelines

- Transitions: Smooth, flowing movements (400ms)

- Easing: Custom cubic-bezier mimicking brush strokes

  ```css
  transition-timing-function: cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1);
  
  
  ```
